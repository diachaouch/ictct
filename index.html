call for papers

We are pleased to invite you to submit original contributions to ICTCT’22 via the Official Submission System (Springer OCS).
Submissions can include technical, experimental, theoretical, conceptual, or survey papers.
All Submissions will be peer- reviewed with reference to relevance, originality, importance and clarity.

SCOPE

The objective of the ICTCT’22 conference is to provide a platform for researchers, academies and experts to meet and share innovative developments in fields such as Telecommunications and Communication Engineering and Networking, Security and Privacy that are spotlight topics interfering in different fields.
Some of these are Computing and Ambient Intelligence, a field that uses engineering principles to address Telecommunicationsneeds.
ICTCT’22 will also include discourses on security because the tendency is to consider both the organizational and the technical aspects of information protection as equally important.
Beyond this, the conference is to expand its coverage in areas such as machine learning and the Internet of Things (IoT), a process performing complex tasks and a combination of mathematics and statistics.
ICTCT’22 offers a balance between innovative industrial approaches and original research work while keeping the readers informed about security techniques, approaches, applications and new technologies.
ICTCT’22 is an opportunity for students, doctors, academics and researchers to open up to the outside world, make connections and collaborate with various domain experts.

The previous edition of ICTCT was held in Polytechnic University of Madrid.The ICTCT’22 will be located in March 18-20, 2022
